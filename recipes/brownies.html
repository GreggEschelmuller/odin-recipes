Quick and Easy Brownies

Description

Quick and easy brownies (taken from all recipes ). These brownies will satisy your sweet tooth, and will only take 30 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 2 cupes white sugar
  • 1.5 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup butter (melted)
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup walnut halves

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch pan.
  2. Mix sugar, flour, melted butter, eggs, cocoa powder, vanilla, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl unitl combined. Spread the batter into the prepared pan. Decorate with walnut halves
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until the top is dry and edges have started to pull away from the sides of the pan, about 20 to 30 minutes; cool before slicing into squares
