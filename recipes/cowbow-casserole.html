Cowbow Casserole

Description

Hearty cobow casserole (taken from all recipes ). This casserole is comfort food at it's best with a creamy filling and crispy tots!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices thick-cut bacon (chopped)
  • 1 cup chopped yellow onion
  • 1 cup chopped red bell pepper
  • 1 pound ground sirloin
  • 8 ounces hot port country sausage
  • 2 teaspoons of chopped garlic
  • 1 (10.75 ounce) condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup frozen corn kernels
  • 1.5 cups shredded extra sharp chees
  • 32 ounce frozen tater tots
  • 2 tablespoons chopped flat-leaf parsley

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) with a rakc in the center position
  2. Heat a large cast iron skillet over medium. Add bacon and cook, stirring often, until brown and crisp (~7 min). Transfer to paper towel lined plate. Save 2 tablespoons of the drippings
  3. Add oinon and and bell pepper to drippings in skillet; cook, stirring occasionally, until softened (~4 min). Increase heat to medium-high and add ground beef and sausage. Cook, stirring, until beef is crumbly, browned, and no longer pink (~6-8 min). Add garlic and cook, stirring constantly, until fragrent (~1 min). Remove from heat and drain fat. Stir in soup, milk, sour cream, salt, and black pepper. Stir in corn, bacon, and 1/2 cup of the cheese.
  4. Sprinkle top eveningly with remaining cup of cheese adn arrange tots in concentric circles over cheese layers.
  5. Bake in oven unitl top is golden brown and beef mixture is bubbly (~35 min).
