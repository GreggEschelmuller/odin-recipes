Thai Coconut Soup

Description

This is a recipe for a delicious thai coconut curry! (taken from all recipes ). This recipe is full of delicious Thai flavours and can be made in only 1 hour! It serves ~8 people.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons grated fresh ginger
  • 2 teaspoons red curry paste
  • 1 stalk lemon grass (minced)
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 3 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon light brown sugar
  • 3 (13.5 ounce) cans coconut milk
  • 1/2 pound fresh shiitake mushrooms (sliced)
  • 1 pound medium shrimp (peeled and deveined)
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
  • salt to taste
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilanto

Directions

  1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add ginger, curry paste, and lemongrass; cook and stir in the hot oil for 1 minute.
  2. Gradually stir in chicken broth, then stir in fish sauce and brown sugar; reduce heat to low and simmer for 15 minutes
  3. Add coconut milk and mushrooms; cook and stir until mushrooms are soft (~5 min)
  4. Add shrimp; cook until no longer translucent (~5 min). Stir in lime juice; season with salt and garnish with cilantro.
